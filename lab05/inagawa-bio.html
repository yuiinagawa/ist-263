Yui Inagawa's Biography

My name is Yui Inagawa and I am a sophomore at Syracuse University. I am a dual major in Newhouse studying television, radio, and film and the iSchool studying informational technology. I have a passion for video editing and doing a lot of “behind the scenes” work for films that are produced. During my freshman year, I produced a documentary for Syracuse NEWS-house where they published my film and wrote an article about it.
