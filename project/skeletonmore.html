Interested in more music like Quinn XCII?

Check out the artists below!


Chelsea Cutler

Chelsea Cutler: 24 year-old American singer-songwriter
Top 3 song recommendations: “Not Okay”, “The Reason”, “Sad Tonight”
Connection to Quinn XCII: Chelsea Cutler and Quinn XCII do many collaborations together such as “Flare Guns”, “Little Things”, “Stay Next to Me”

Ayokay

Ayokay: 29 year-old American producer and DJ
Top 3 song recommendations: “Kings of Summer”, “Stay with me”, “Queen”
Connection to Quinn XCII: Ayokay is Quinn XCII’s highschool friend who eventually became his primary producer

Jeremy Zucker

Jeremy Zucker: 25 year-old American singer-songwriter
Top 3 song recommendations: “All the Kids are Depressed”, “Comethru”, “Talk is Overrated”
Connection to Quinn XCII: Jeremy has a song with Quinn XCII called “Doris Terrace” and he has a couple songs with Chelsea Cutler such as, “You Were Good to Me” and “Please”
